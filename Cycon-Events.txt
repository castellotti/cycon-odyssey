COG Engine Game Module - Event List File
-----------------------------------------
Grammar Last Modified : 1999.04.20
Event List Last Modified : 1999.08.12

Events are used to descibe what can happen in a game. Events are necessary
 when manipulation and interacting with Items and Obstructions. 

All Events must adhere to the following EBNF Grammar:

<Action> <Object> [<Preposition> <Object>]
[ '('Requires [! | '('Not')'] <Requirement>')' ]
{ ( and | or ) '('Requires [! | '('Not')'] <Requirement>')' } ->
	<Effect> {and <Effect>};

Actions:
Get
Drop
( and any User-Defined Verb in the Verb Text File)

Refs:          // References to Objects
'(' int ')'    // Reference to Object's Number
'[' string ']' // Reference to Object's Name

Prepositions:
with
on
from
to
etc. (Any word or string of characters, without any spaces)

Objects:
Item<Ref>
Obstruction<Ref>

Requirements:
PlayerPoints'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerExp'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerHP'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerMP'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerStr'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerIQ'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerDex'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerAgil'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerCharisma'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerArmorLevel'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
PlayerCurrentWeight'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
Room<Ref>IsCurrentRoom
Room<Ref>HasVisited
Item<Ref>InInventory
Item<Ref>IsEquipped
Item<Ref>ExistsInRoom<Ref>
Item<Ref>Weight'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
Item<Ref>Bulk'['<Comparison>'](' int ')'
Obstruction<Ref>ExistsInRoom<Ref>
Obstruction<Ref>ExistsInRoom<Ref>Direction<Ref>
Obstruction<Ref>IsVisible

Effects:
Adds Item<Ref> <Preposition> <ItemLocation>
Adds Obstruction<Ref> <Preposition> Room<Ref>Direction<Ref>
Removes Item<Ref> <Preposition> <ItemLocation>
Removes Obstruction<Ref> <Preposition> Room<Ref>Direction<Ref>
Modifies Player <PlayerAttributes>
Modifies Room<Ref> <RoomAttributes>
Modifies Item<Ref> <ItemAttributes>
Modifies Obstruction<Ref> <ObstructionAttributes>
TextMessage'[' string Message ']' 
GraphicMessage'['URL, int, int ']' // [Graphic URL, X-coordinate, Y-coordinate]

ItemLocations:
Inventory
CurrentRoom
Room<Ref>

PlayerAttributes:
PlayerPoints'['<Expression>'](' int ')'
PlayerExp'['<Expression>'](' int ')'
PlayerHP'['<Expression>'](' int ')'

Get Item[Rock] ->
 Removes Item[Rock] from CurrentRoom
 and Adds Item(30) to CurrentRoom
 and Adds Item[Dagger] to CurrentRoom
 and Modifies Player PlayerPoints[+](5)
 and TextMessage[Lifting up the rock, you find a dagger hidden underneath!]
 and GraphicMessage[Images/ItemTemplate(Green).jpg, 0, 0];

Move Item[Rock] ->
 Removes Item[Rock] from CurrentRoom
 and Adds Item(30) to CurrentRoom
 and Adds Item[Dagger] to CurrentRoom
 and Modifies Player PlayerPoints[+](5)
 and TextMessage[Lifting up the rock, you find a dagger hidden underneath!]
 and GraphicMessage[Images/ItemTemplate(Green).jpg, 0, 0];

Attack Obstruction[Savage] with Item[Dagger] ->
 Removes Obstruction[Savage] from Room[Savage]Direction[West]
 and Removes Obstruction[Savage] from Room(7)Direction[East]
 and Adds Item[Arrow] to CurrentRoom
 and Adds Item[Bow] to CurrentRoom
 and Modifies Player PlayerPoints[+](10)
 and TextMessage[With lightning reflexes you lunge at the savage with your blade. Your arc falls wide however,
 and your target is left unhurt. Frightened by your actions, the native inhabitant runs off into the forset brush,
 leaving behind a hand-crafted bow and an arrow.];

Combine Item[Rope] with Item[Arrow] -> 
 Removes Item[Rope] from Inventory
 and Removes Item[Arrow] from Inventory
 and Adds Item[Rope Tied to Arrow] to Inventory
 and TextMessage[You tie the rope onto the end of the arrow.];

Split Item[Rope Tied to Arrow] -> 
 Removes Item[Rope Tied to Arrow] from Inventory
 and Adds Item[Rope] to Inventory
 and Adds Item[Arrow] to Inventory
 and TextMessage[You untie the rope from the arrow.];

Use Item[Rope Tied to Arrow] on Item[Tree Stump]
 (Requires Item[Bow]InInventory) ->
 Removes Item[Rope Tied to Arrow] from Inventory
 and Adds Item[Arrow (with Rope) shot into Stump] to Room(129)
 and Adds Item[Arrow (with Rope) shot into Stump] to Room(104)
 and Modifies Room(129) DirectionObject[Up]ToWhichRoom(104)
 and Modifies Room(104) DirectionObject[Down]ToWhichRoom(129)
 and Removes Item[Tree Stump] from Room(129)
 and Removes Item[Tree Stump] from Room(104)
 and TextMessage[You take the arrow that you have tied your rope onto and pull it back on your bow. After taking
 aim at the tree stump, on the top of the cliff high above the ground, you release the arrow. Your shot is clean
 and diectly on target, lodging into the tree stump. You can now climb Up or Down the rope along the cliff.];

Get Item[Arrow (with Rope) shot into Stump] ->
 Adds Item[Rope Tied to Arrow] to Inventory
 and Modifies Room(129) DirectionObject[Up]ToWhichRoom(0)
 and Modifies Room(104) DirectionObject[Down]ToWhichRoom(0)
 and Adds Item[Tree Stump] to Room(129)
 and Adds Item[Tree Stump] to Room(104)
 and Removes Item[Arrow (with Rope) shot into Stump] from Room(129)
 and Removes Item[Arrow (with Rope) shot into Stump] from Room(104);

Combine Item[Rope] with Item[Hook] -> Adds Item[Grappling Hook] to Inventory;

Split Item[Grappling Hook] -> Adds Item[Rope] to Inventory and Adds Item[Hook] to Inventory;
